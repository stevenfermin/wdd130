Rafting Company

Our purpose is to make fun and unforgettable experiences for the participants while they're practicing this sport with their teams, create new professionals and a way to gather the family in the same place.

History

In 1895 we started providing a fun and different way to play in the water, since that time, a lot of people joined and make new experiences and play to be faster with their team, since 1945 we've created the competitive section, where the teams shows their ability with and their best strategy to be the the first in the pole in each section of rafting.
